Bam Birdie Boo



A different bird game with shields, poop and a cat. Avoid the ground, bamboos and curious cat.
Collect crystals for shields to protect birdie from bamboo tips but not the bamboo.
Eat moths for poop ammo. Tap on cat to shoot poop.

If bird dies, you have a choice of OK or CONTINUE.
OK - Starts with beginning score of each attempt. (no ads)
CONTINUE - auto saves and continues game with your latest score if you earn 20+ points per attempt. (with ads)
